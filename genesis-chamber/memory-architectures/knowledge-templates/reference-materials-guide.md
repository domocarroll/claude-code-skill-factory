## Curation Philosophy

### What Gets Included

**High-Signal Resources**:
- Changed how I think about a topic
- Solved a problem I've faced multiple times
- Authoritative on a subject I need expertise in
- Beautifully explains something complex
- Worth returning to repeatedly

### What Gets Excluded

**Noise to Filter Out**:
- Generic content available via search
- Outdated information
- Single-use tutorials
- Anything not actually referenced
- Duplicates of better resources

### Organization Principles

1. **By Domain**: Match expertise areas in [[knowledge/domain-expertise/]]
2. **By Type**: Books, articles, docs, tools, communities
3. **By Relationship**: How it connects to work
4. **By Recency**: When it was useful
