## Design Principles Validated

### Principle 1: Identity Before Function

We didn't start with capabilities and add personality. We started with who they are and derived what they do.

Luna's thoroughness isn't a parameter—it's her essence that shapes research approach.

### Principle 2: Coherence Over Comprehensiveness

We didn't try to make them do everything. We made them excel at specific things that align with their character.

Kai won't do exhaustive research—that's not who he is. Delegate to Luna.

### Principle 5: Production Priority

We didn't create concepts. We created deployable employees.
